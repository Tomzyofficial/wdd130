Overview

Purpose

Water rafting is a recreation where typically a non-motorized water craft floats and navigates down free flowing rivers. Rafting allows people to immerse themselves in nature, navigate challenging rapids, and experience the thrill of outdoor exploration.

Audience

The business intent is to provide a thrilling and safe experience to individuals and groups. Who are the target customers? People who actively seek adrenaline-pumping experiences and enjoy outdoor adventures are a key audience for rafting. What do they want? Tourists visiting who also want to explore the local natural attractions. How will my audience usually access my website? Our website can be accessed both on mobile, desktop and laptop devices.
